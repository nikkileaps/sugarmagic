xatlas.js / xatlas.wasm

https://github.com/repalash/xatlas.js -- a WASM build of xatlas
(https://github.com/jpcy/xatlas, MIT), the mesh parameterization
library. Used by sugarmagic to generate the PAINT UV channel
(TEXCOORD_1) for imported assets (Plan 068.8). Loaded at runtime by
the xatlas-three UVUnwrapper worker; update by re-copying from the
xatlasjs dist after a version bump.
